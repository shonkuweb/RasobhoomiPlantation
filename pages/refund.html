Refund Policy

Rasobhoomi follows a strict no-refund policy due to the nature of live plants. Once an order is placed and payment is completed, the amount paid for the plant is non-refundable. This applies to all plants, including small plants, large drum plants, and potted plants.

Under no circumstances will Rasobhoomi issue a monetary refund, whether in cash, bank transfer, UPI, or any other digital payment method. This policy exists because plants are perishable living items that require careful handling and cannot be resold once dispatched or delivered.

In cases where a plant is damaged or dies within 15 days of delivery, Rasobhoomi does not provide a refund. Instead, the customer is eligible to receive a replacement plant. The replacement will be of the same type as originally ordered, subject to availability. If the same plant is not available, the customer may choose another plant of similar value.

While Rasobhoomi bears the responsibility of providing the replacement plant, the shipping or courier charges for the replacement must be paid by the customer. No refund will be issued for shipping charges already paid.

Refunds are also not applicable if the customer changes their mind after placing an order, selects the wrong plant, or is dissatisfied due to personal preference. Customers are encouraged to carefully review plant details and use the video call selection option (for large plants) before finalizing their purchase.

By making a purchase from Rasobhoomi, the customer acknowledges and agrees to this refund policy and understands that plant prices are never refundable.

ALL THE APPROVED EXCHANGE AND REPLACEMENT WILL BE COMPLETED WITHIN 7 BUSINESS DAYS, REFUNDS WILL BE CREDITED BACK TO THE ORIGINAL PAYMENT METHOD IN 7 BUSINESS DAYS.
